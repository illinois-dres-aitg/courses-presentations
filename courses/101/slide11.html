Disability Barrier: Structure

Landmarks
Identifies major sections of content on the page.
Visual layout and alignment is often used to visually identify sections of content.
Screen readers can navigate the landmark structure of the page.
Headings
Identifies the structure of content within a section of content or a document.
Often used to label landmarks on the page.
Screen readers can navigate the landmark structure of the page.
Page Title
Identifies the individual page within a website/application.
Identifies the website/application be used.
Read by screen readers when the page is first loaded.
