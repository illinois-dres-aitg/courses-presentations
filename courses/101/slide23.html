Summary

  • Everyone has different abilities.
  • Universal design reduces or eliminates need for accommodations.
  • Accessibility is the law and is a civil right.
  • Policies, implementation plans and dedicated staff are need to manage accessibility.
  • Accessibility is a team effort, no one person or group can make an organization accessible, everyone plays a part.
  • People need to know their role and responsibilities for accessibility.
