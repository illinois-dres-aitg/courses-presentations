Success Criteria
  • Real requirements of WCAG.
  • Three levels of importance: A, AA and AAA.
  • Meeting level A and AA requirements are used by most regulatory standards as the mimimum for conformance.
  • Most compliance standards only require A and AA requirements.
  • None of the techniques are considered normative, but they label some sufficent and others advisory techniques.
