Summary

  • The orginal purpose of the web was for people to share information and collaborate on creating new information.
  • As people age likelihood of severe disability greatly increases.
  • Universal design helps all users access and interact with information, including people with disabilities.
  • The heart of the web is interoperability and open standards.
  • HTML5 and ARIA specifications are leading to interoperability for people with disabilities.
  • More people must understand accessibility and accessibility standards.
  • Accessibility must be integrated into web development and authoring environments.
