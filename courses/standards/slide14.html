Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1

Important Features

  • International accessibility standard, including the United States Federal Section 508 requirements for web accessibility.
  • Technology agnostic, so understanding requires knowledge of features of specific technologies.
  • Requirements identify the goals for accessibility, not the techniques.
  • Techniques referenced by WCAG are not normative or comprehensive, but are deisgned to help people understand the requirement and feasibility of meeting the requirement.
  • Supporting references:
    • How to Meet WCAG 2.1
    • Understanding WCAG 2.1
    • Techniques for WCAG 2.1
