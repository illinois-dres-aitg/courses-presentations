World Wide Web Consortium

  • World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)
  • Over 400 member organizations representing industry, governments and academic interests.
  • Develop open and royality free standards:
    • Hypertext Markup Language (HTML) Specifications
    • Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) Specifications
    • Portable Network Graphics (PNG) Specifications
    • Scallable Vector Graphics (SVG) Specifications
    • Mathematical Markup Language (MathML) Specifications
  • Interopability through at least 2 indepdent implementations
  • Automated testing of technologies using the speficications.
