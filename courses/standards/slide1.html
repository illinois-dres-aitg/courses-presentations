Hello, my name is Jon Gunderson from the University of Illinois and I am the instructor for this course. This slide outlines the major goals of the universal design and web standards video. The set of slides in this presentation will be divided into two videos, the first part is about disability, universal design and accessibility laws. The second video will focus on the W3C web accessibility standards. This two videos should help you learn about and understand. The original purpose of the web. The prevalence and types of disabilities as we age. Introduce the concept of Universal Design for the web. Universal Design principles improve the usability of the web for everyone, including people with disabilities. Web accessibility is a right, not just a nice thing to do. Why the use of web standards is important for creating accessible web content. And how W3C standards support accessible design.
