"Rehab" Program becomes Official Part of Illinois

  • GI Bill for World War 2 veterans.
  • Galesburg VA in 1948 had UI extension courses for soliders.
  • Soldiers in using wheelchairs wanted to continue their studies at Univeristy of Illinois.
  • Initially rejected for admission to Illinois based solely on their disability and their perceived inability to be able to live independently on campus.
  • Soldiers lobbied the state government and university administration to be admitted.
  • Had the highest grade point average of all the houses on campus during their first year on campus.
  • After 6 years the "Rehab" program offically changed from experimental (e.g. unfundex) to become an offical part (funded) program of the University of Illinois.
